10) Recommended Replit content structure

Since you’re building in Replit, this content should not live hardcoded inside components one section at a time. Better structure:
	•	content/es.ts
	•	content/en.ts
	•	content/pt.ts

And inside each file:
	•	nav
	•	home
	•	about
	•	services
	•	capabilities
	•	contact
	•	footer
	•	seo

That will make the multilingual version much cleaner.

My blunt recommendation

For V1, do not overbuild the website.

Keep it to:
	•	Home
	•	About
	•	Services
	•	Capabilities
	•	Contact

That is enough to look serious, premium, and credible.
Trying to add too much too early usually makes the site feel generic and diluted.

Next step should be turning this into a Replit-ready structured content object in code, with:
	•	Spanish
	•	English
	•	Portuguese
	•	clean keys for every section and button.
